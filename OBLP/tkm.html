To Kill a Mockingbird

To Kill a Mockingbird

by Harper Lee

Rating: 4.8/5

Set in the Deep South during the 1930s, this novel follows young Scout Finch as her father, Atticus, defends a black man falsely accused of rape. Through Scout’s eyes, we explore powerful themes of racial injustice, moral growth, and compassion. A timeless classic about empathy and human dignity.
