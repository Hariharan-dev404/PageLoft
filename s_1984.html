1984

by George Orwell

Rating: 4.7/5

1984 is a dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society ruled by Big Brother. The story follows Winston Smith, a government employee who begins to question the regime and seeks truth and freedom. Through powerful themes of surveillance, propaganda, and repression, Orwell delivers a haunting warning about the dangers of absolute power and loss of individuality.
